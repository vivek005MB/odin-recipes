Boiled Eggs


Description

Recipe for perfectly boiling the eggs.

Ingredients

  • 6 Whole Eggs
  • Table salt
  • Vineger
  • Cold water

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil.
  2. Add salt and Vineger.
  3. Boil for 12 minutes
  4. Transfer the eggs to the cold water (you can use ice water)
  5. Peel the eggs once they are cooled.
