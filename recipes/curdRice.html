Curd rice


Description

Recipe for curd rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (curd)
  • 0.25 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the rice and cool it to the room temperature.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked rice, plain yogurt, and milk. Mix well until the rice is evenly coated.
  3. Heat ghee in a small pan over medium heat.
  4. Add mustard seeds to the hot ghee and check out for seeds to be poped.
  5. Add finely chopped green chilies, grated ginger, and curry leaves to the pan. Saute for a few seconds.
  6. Pour the tempered mixture over the rice and yogurt mixture. Mix well.
  7. Add salt to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and prefer serving chilled.
