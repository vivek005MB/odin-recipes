Kheer


Description

Recipe for easy to make kheer(rice pudding kinda!).

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 5 roasted and chopped almonds
  • 2 cup of full fat milk.
  • 0.25 cup raisins
  • 0.5 tablespoons of cardamom

Steps

  1. In saucepan, boil the milk with sugar.
  2. Add rice, cardamom, raisins to the mixture and cook them for 20 minutes till rice are tender.
  3. Garnish with almonds.
